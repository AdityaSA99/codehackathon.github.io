Code Hackathon

We are a team of students in GITAM that aim to promote coding as being fun, challenging and most of all #NotBoring.

In collaboration with the department of CSE, we organize frequent coding competitions called 'CODE HACKATHONS' on a bi-weekly basis on Saturday afternoons (except for during important college events). We may on occasion also take classes to discuss and try out various techniques and challenges to further enhance your skills.
